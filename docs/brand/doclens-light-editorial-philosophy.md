# Light Editorial

Light Editorial treats precision as something calm rather than severe. The symbol should feel like a page held in soft daylight: measured, legible, and meticulously crafted, with every corner and curve suggesting patient refinement instead of force. It belongs to a product that studies documents closely, so the mark should communicate observation and structure through shape alone, not through decorative excess.

The visual language relies on pale mineral surfaces, restrained ink blues, and a quiet hierarchy of whites and fogged greys. Warm accents appear only as a whisper, never as a dominant voice. This palette should feel meticulously balanced, as if each tone were selected after countless tiny adjustments. The result must read as expert work at first glance: light, deliberate, and undeniably polished.

Form carries the story. A document silhouette should remain present, but softened into something elegant rather than literal clip art. The "lens" idea should not become a cartoon magnifying glass; it should feel integrated into the document itself, like focus emerging from the page. The folded corner introduces intelligence and paper memory, while the circular focus shape suggests inspection, clarity, and recovery of detail.

Scale behavior matters as much as the full composition. The mark must be painstakingly resolved so it stays recognizable in a navbar, on a presentation slide, and inside a favicon. Small-size clarity is part of the craftsmanship. The symbol should therefore avoid noise, preserve strong silhouette contrast, and hold together when reduced to a handful of pixels. The final impression should be the product of deep expertise and obsessive attention to proportion.

Typography and layout around the mark should stay spare and editorial. The icon does the conceptual work; the wordmark simply anchors it. Everything should appear meticulously crafted, quietly premium, and built by someone who spent a long time removing anything unnecessary. The whole system should feel like a document tool that values judgment, cleanliness, and exactness.
